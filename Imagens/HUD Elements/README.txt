# Dark Survival HUD Elements – Free Asset Pack

### 📦 **Description:**
This asset pack includes essential HUD elements with a dark, hand-drawn style. Perfect for survival, RPG, or dark-themed games. All files are provided in PNG format with transparent backgrounds.

---

### 📂 **Content:**

- **Alert Indicator:** 256x256 px (Warning icon with rugged, ink-style design)
- **Resource Counter:** 512x128 px (Resource counter for wood, stones, or other collectibles)
- **Notification Banner:** 1024x512 px (Event notification banner with dark, distressed texture)
- **Stamina Bar:** 1024x512 px (stamina bar with rough, hand-drawn look)

---

### 🚀 **Suggested Use:**
- Use the Alert Indicator for critical warnings or damage alerts.
- The Resource Counter can track collected items, such as wood, stones, or food.
- The Notification Banner is perfect for event pop-ups or special announcements.
- The Health & Stamina Bar is ideal for managing player status in survival or RPG games.

---

### ⚡ **Compatibility:**
- PNG (Transparent, high resolution, no compression)
- Compatible with Unity, Godot, and other 2D game engines.
